What is A Friend

Karen A. Cina

A true friend is a gift,
Received from God above
He starts out with sincerity,
Then adds an abundance of love.

A friend will always be there,
No matter what you do.
Willing to stand by your side,
They laugh and cry with you too.

A friend is someone who knows you,
Both inside and out.
They are never one to judge,
And support you with no doubt.

A friend will never leave you,
Even when times are bad.
You may not always agree,
But you never leave each other mad.

A friend will share their secrets,
With faith that they are kept.
They'll give you whatever they've got,
Even if they have nothing left.

Author: Karen A. Cina
